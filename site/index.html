About us

We are Beatrice and Joseph, and we are thrilled to invite you to celebrate our wedding! After years of adventures together, we said "yes" and are now counting down the days to our big celebration.

We chose the Château de Mairy for its timeless charm, its beautiful gardens, and the warmth of the Champagne countryside. Nothing would make this day more special than having you there to share it with us.

We cannot wait to raise a glass (of Champagne, naturally) with each and every one of you!

How to get there

We will celebrate at Château de Mairy, 51240 Mairy-sur-Marne, a 17th-century Monument Historique set within 10 hectares of gardens and woodland in the heart of Champagne.

From Paris

~1h40 by car / ~1h40 by train

By car

  • Take the A4 motorway east towards Reims / Metz
  • Exit at Châlons-en-Champagne
  • Follow signs to Mairy-sur-Marne (~10 min)

By train

  • TGV from Paris-Est to Châlons-en-Champagne (~1h30)
  • Taxi or shuttle from the station (~10 min)

Book on SNCF Connect · Trainline

From Strasbourg

~3h by car / ~2h30 by train

By car

  • Take the A4 motorway west towards Paris
  • Exit at Châlons-en-Champagne
  • Follow signs to Mairy-sur-Marne

By train

  • Strasbourg to Châlons-en-Champagne (~2h30, connection in Reims)
  • Taxi or shuttle from the station (~10 min)

Book on SNCF Connect · Trainline

Where to stay

The château is located near Châlons-en-Champagne, with Reims also within easy reach. Here are our recommendations:

Châlons-en-Champagne

~10 min from the venue

  • The closest town with a wide range of hotels
  • TGV train station for easy arrival/departure
  • Several restaurants and bars in the town centre

Reims

~45 min from the venue

  • Larger city with more hotel options
  • Worth a visit for its cathedral and Champagne houses
  • Connected by TGV to Paris in 45 min

More specific hotel recommendations will follow. If you need help booking, don't hesitate to reach out!
