How to get there

We will celebrate at Château de Mairy, 51240 Mairy-sur-Marne, a 17th-century Monument Historique set within 10 hectares of gardens and woodland in the heart of Champagne.

From Paris

~1h40 by car: take the A4 motorway east towards Reims/Metz. Exit at Châlons-en-Champagne, then follow signs to Mairy-sur-Marne (~10 min). By train: TGV Paris-Est → Châlons-en-Champagne (~1h30), then taxi/shuttle (~10 min).

From Strasbourg

~3h by car: take the A4 motorway west towards Paris. Exit at Châlons-en-Champagne, then follow signs to Mairy-sur-Marne. By train: Strasbourg → Châlons-en-Champagne (~2h30 with connection in Reims), then taxi/shuttle.

On site

Parking available at the château. The domain is spread across 10 hectares with gardens and woodland.

Hotels

We recommend staying in Châlons-en-Champagne (10 min away) or Reims (45 min). More details to follow.
