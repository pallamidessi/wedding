How to get there

We will celebrate at Villa del Sogno, Via Roma 10, 22021 Bellagio, Lake Como.

By plane

The nearest airports are Milan Malpensa (MXP), Milan Linate (LIN), and Bergamo (BGY). From there, take a train or rental car.

By train

Reach Varenna-Esino station from Milan Centrale, then take the ferry to Bellagio.

By car

Driving from Milan takes around 1h 30m. Parking will be available near the venue.

Hotels

We recommend staying in Bellagio, Varenna, or Menaggio. Book early as September is a busy period.
